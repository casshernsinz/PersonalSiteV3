Work Experience

Lumiere Place Casino & Hotel

I worked at Lumiere Place Casino and Hotel Lumiere for a total of four years as a maintenance technician. My duties were to assess areas where maintenance was required and to apply appropriate fixes or touch-ups where necessary; anything from a hole in drywall needing to be filled in to checking the building's chillers and boilers.

During my time at Lumiere (now known as, El Dorado, as of late 2019) I also became a certified pool technician and had been in charge of maintaining chemical balance of the swimming pool and the hot tub. It was also my duty to inspect and regulate the pool and hot tub sand filters to make sure they were operating at full capacity. This included duties such as cleaning the filters themselves along with the routine backwashing, winterizing, etc.

I have done plumbing as part of my job and have had to run heavy machinery to repair broken pipes and to inspect and clean any clogged drainpipes.

My job also consisted of having to give clear and tactful feedback to various departments on the property. It was necessary, at times, to have to deliver news that certain areas of the property needed to be shut-down in order to perform proper maintenance. This extended to the customers as well and occasionally resulted in the temporary removal or reassignment of customers in order to pursue necessary measures to ensure the safety of everyone and anyone on the property.
